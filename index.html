FAQ

How does cede.store work?
Cede.store is a non-custodial wallet for your CEX API keys. It stores your API keys directly on your machine and lets you interact with your CEX. Everything is trustless, it means that at no moment, you have to trust a third-party.
Do I need KYC to use cede.store?
No, you don't. You will connect to your CEX through cede.store and to DeFi through your DeFi wallets. You will only need to do the classic KYC process on your CEX beforehand.
What's a vault?
A vault lets you group together a bunch of CEX. You can manage the permissions you give to a dApp. For example, if you don't trust a dApp, you can give access only to your tracking vault so the dApp will not be able to initiate a trade or a withdraw. You could create also a trading vault or a withdrawal vault.
How much does cede.store cost?
Cede.store is free of use. Some paid features will come later.
How many CEX I can add to cede.store?
As many as you want!

How can I transfer my CEX token to my DeFi address?
You need to be connected to cede.store. You can go to the Portal page on cede.link. Then, you will need to fill all the inputs.
How can I add my CEX accounts to cede.store?
You will need to generate API keys on your CEX with the permissions that you want. Then, you need to go to the vault settings on cede.store to setup your keys.
